Lasagna Recipe

That's a good looking tin!

What makes a lasagna great? The cheese? The meat? The layers? What about all of the above? That's this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomatoes
  • Parmiggiano Reggiano
  • Mozzarella (enough for 3 layers)
  • Trimmed Brisket or Chuck roast, sliced into 3in cubes
  • Bottle of good red winw
  • Diced Oninons
  • Diced Fennel (1 head)
  • Diced Garlic (about 3 heads)
  • Bay Leaf (5)
  • Basil
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Equipment List

  • 9x13in baking tin
  • 6-8 quart dutch oven
  • Wine Corkscrew
  • 8in chef's knife
  • Ladle
  • Prep bowls
